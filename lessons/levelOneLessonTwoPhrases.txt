Lesson 2: Phrases

English              Turkish
hello                     = merhaba
good bye              = hoşça kal
see you                 = görüşürüz
how are you          = nasılsın
nice to meet you   = memnun oldum
please                    = lütfen
thank you             = teşekkür ederim
you're welcome      = rica ederim
thanks                   = teşekkürler
excuse me              = affedersin
sorry                   = pardon
welcome                = hoş geliniz
(reply to welcome) = hoş bulduk
bon appetit           = afiyet olsun
good morning       = günaydın
good evening        = iyi akşamlar
good night            = iyi geceler
good luck             = iyi şanslar

This may seem intimidating at first but take it slow.
Feel free to learn five words a day then take the test in a few days
When you feel ready, see what you learned!
